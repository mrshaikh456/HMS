Welcome to HMS Environmental Engineering

At HMS Environmental Engineering, we are committed to safeguarding our water resources and advancing sustainable infrastructure through innovative engineering. Our goal is to transform water and wastewater challenges into clean, efficient, and environmentally responsible solutions.

In addition to turnkey project delivery, we specialize in plant maintenance and offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) to ensure uninterrupted performance. Our team also excels in upgrading conventional manual systems into modern, fully automated operations, enhancing efficiency and long-term reliability.

With deep technical expertise and a client-centric approach, we deliver comprehensive services—from concept, design, and installation to testing, commissioning, and handover. Whether it’s a municipal sewage system or an industrial RO plant, every project reflects our dedication to precision, reliability, and ecological balance.

We begin by optimizing what’s already in place and build from the ground up—delivering innovative, efficient, and sustainable water solutions at every stage of the lifecycle.

“Designing today’s solutions for tomorrow’s environment.”
